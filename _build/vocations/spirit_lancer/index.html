Spirit Lancer (Support)

Overview

The Spirit Lancer is a versatile melee class in Dragon's Dogma Online, adept at combining spear techniques with spiritual energy to empower allies and devastate foes. This vocation thrives on fluid combat, blending crowd control, healing, and powerful single-target strikes.

Key Characteristics

  • Wields a spear infused with spiritual energy for both melee and ranged attacks.
  • Balances offensive power with support and healing abilities.
  • Excels at crowd control through wide-reaching spear techniques.
  • Requires agility and precision to capitalize on versatile combat options.

Core Combat Mechanics

  • Aurom Fang: Strike twice, knocking light enemies into the air and following them
  • Wall Glasta: Place an energy reserve. It grants health regeneration and a 15% defense buff to any ally who enters it.
  • Col Storm: Release a series of quick cuts by rotating the side of the boom to the side.
  • Meteor shower: The attack starts with a backslash attack, then a vertical slash and ends with an aerial attack that slams the spear into the ground.

Recommended Play Style

  • Engage enemies at medium range to leverage the spear's reach and precision.
  • Support your team by healing allies and enhancing their defenses in critical moments.
  • Control crowds with sweeping spear strikes while targeting key threats for elimination.
  • Adapt to combat scenarios by alternating between offensive and support roles as needed.
