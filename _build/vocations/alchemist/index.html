Alchemist (Tank)

Overview

The Alchemist is a unique hybrid class in Dragon's Dogma Online, blending melee combat with arcane alchemy. This vocation utilizes a magical cauldron to unleash powerful transmutations, summon constructs, and manipulate the battlefield with both offensive and defensive capabilities.

Key Characteristics

  • Combines melee strikes with alchemical spells and transformations.
  • Utilizes a magical cauldron for crafting and summoning constructs.
  • Excels in crowd control and environmental manipulation.
  • Offers versatility through offensive, defensive, and support abilities.

Core Combat Mechanics

  • Transmutation Strike: A melee attack that channels alchemical energy for explosive results.
  • Construct Creation: Summons golem-like entities to fight alongside the Alchemist.
  • Barrier Field: Creates a protective alchemical shield to block enemy attacks.
  • Elemental Infusion: Imbues attacks with elemental properties to exploit enemy weaknesses.

Recommended Play Style

  • Utilize constructs and barriers to control the flow of battle and protect allies.
  • Adapt to encounters by leveraging the Alchemist's diverse toolkit of spells and transformations.
  • Engage enemies at close to mid-range, combining melee strikes with alchemical abilities.
  • Strategically manipulate the battlefield to gain an advantage in both offense and defense.
